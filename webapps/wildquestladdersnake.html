🎮 Board Guide

🪜
Ladder
Climb Forward! When you land on a ladder square, you automatically climb up to a higher square. Gain +5 bonus points for climbing!
🐍
Snake
Slide Back! When you land on a snake head, you slide down to its tail. Use your Shield power-up to block the slide, or hope your character has snake immunity!
⭐
Bonus Star
Double Luck! Land here to roll the dice twice next turn — roll one extra time! You also earn a Shield power-up to help block snakes.
🔄
Challenge Square
Extra Reward! Landing here earns you a Sabotage power-up to send a rival back 3 squares. Challenge squares test your skill!
🎯
HOTS Square
Double Points! Land on a HOTS square and all question points are doubled! Answer correctly and earn 2x the normal bonus. HOTS = Higher Order Thinking Skills.
🤝
Kolaborasi
Team Bonus! A collaboration square where teamwork is rewarded. All groups benefit with +3 bonus points when anyone lands here!
⏭️
Free Pass
Skip Questions! Land here to earn a free pass. Use it to skip your next question and move forward without answering!
□
Normal Square
Safe Zone! Regular squares are safe. Land here and get ready to answer your next question. No special effects — just a moment to prepare!
