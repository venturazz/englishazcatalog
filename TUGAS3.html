📚 Standardized Reading Text

Pelajari anatomi teks deskriptif utuh berikut ini. Perhatikan perbedaan informasi umum (Identification) dan penjabaran ciri khusus (Description).

A. Identification (General Information)

🟢 Paragraph 1: Overview & History

The Taj Mahal is an internationally renowned ivory-white marble mausoleum situated elegantly on the southern bank of the Yamuna River in Agra, India. It was commissioned in 1631 by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan to honor his beloved wife, Mumtaz Mahal. Today, it stands as the ultimate epitome of Mughal architecture and is universally recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage site.

B. Description (Specific Features)

🔵 Paragraph 2: Structural Dome

Architecturally, the monument showcases an unparalleled architectural scale. The main edifice is constructed from white marble block tiles and topped by an enormous central onion dome reaching 73 meters in height. This giant dome is flanked dynamically by four smaller cupolas.

🔵 Paragraph 3: Engineering Minarets

At the four terminal corners of the square foundation plinth, four tall and slender minarets stand detached. Interestingly, these minarets are engineered to lean slightly outward as a deliberate safety precaution against severe seismic earthquakes, ensuring they collapse away from the tomb.

🔵 Paragraph 4: Inlay Ornamentation

The exterior ornamentation showcases the masterpiece of the Mughal artistic style. The walls are embellished with refined Arabic calligraphy, complex geometric layouts, and the accurate pietra dura technique utilizing inlaid semi-precious gemstones.

🔵 Paragraph 5: Surrounding Landscape

Surrounding the majestic mausoleum is a sprawling, meticulously arranged Mughal charbagh garden. A long, linear reflecting pool runs through the center of the lawn, creating a pristine reflection that enhances its romantic atmosphere.
